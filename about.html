About Florendex

Who we are and why we do this

What is Florendex?

Florendex is an online index dedicated to documenting flowering plant species from around the world. Our goal is to make botanical information accessible to everyone — whether you're a professional botanist, a student, or just someone who spotted an interesting flower on a walk and wants to know more.

We cover everything from common garden plants to rare wildflowers, providing descriptions, photographs, and conservation information for each species.

Why does it matter?

Flowering plants make up the majority of plant life on Earth and are essential to almost every ecosystem. They support pollinators like bees and butterflies, provide food and medicine, and play a huge role in regulating our climate. Despite this, thousands of species are threatened by habitat loss, climate change, and invasive species.

By documenting these species and making that information freely available, we hope to raise awareness and support conservation efforts worldwide.

How we work

Our entries are written and reviewed by volunteers with backgrounds in botany, ecology, and conservation. We draw on published scientific literature and verified field observations. If you'd like to contribute, get in touch through our contact page.
