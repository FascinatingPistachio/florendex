Featured Flora

Summer Snowflake

Leucojum aestivum

Leucojum aestivum, commonly called the summer snowflake, is a plant species widely cultivated as an ornamental. Found across Europe and western Asia.

Cornish Heath

Erica vagans

Erica vagans, the Cornish heath or wandering heath, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae, native to Ireland, Cornwall, western France and Spain.

Spring Gentian

Gentiana verna

Gentiana verna, the spring gentian, is one of the smallest members of its family, normally only growing to a height of a few centimetres. Known for its striking blue flowers.

Our Mission

Florendex exists to document and celebrate the diversity of flowering plant species across the world. We believe that understanding flowers is the first step toward protecting them - and the pollinators that depend on them.
